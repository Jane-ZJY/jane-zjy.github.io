Handheld Bioprinter

Shape-memory hydrogel, Skin Repair

This project is another current project I am working on, at Harvard Medical School. A self-made handheld printer will be used for skin repair. We use a kind of hydrogel with porous structure fabricating by a novel method to be printed onto the skin damaged area. Sepcifically, the hydrogel has superior properties, such as shape memory, toughness, big porous for good cell growth, etc. Read more...
